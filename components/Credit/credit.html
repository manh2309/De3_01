Welcome to Credit Resources

When you apply for a mortgage, your loan officer will obtain and review your credit report. If your loan officer determines that you can benefit from credit score improvement, you may be referred to work with Fairway's internal Creditools team. One of our highly skilled credit analysts will be assigned to review your credit report and draft a credit improvement action plan for you. There is no cost to you to do so.
